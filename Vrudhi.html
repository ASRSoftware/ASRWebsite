VRUDHI is a Multidisciplinary Institute for patient care and research pursuit in the frontier area of Mental Health. The centre was established in the year 2003 by likeminded professionals. The centre provides state of the art diagnostic, therapeutic & remedial aid to meet varied requirements of all needing mental health care i.e.: from childhood to old age.
